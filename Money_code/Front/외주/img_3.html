Food

The side dish that Koreans enjoy most when drinking is pork belly. Similar to the barbecue in the West, Koreans eat pork on a frying pan by baking it on the spot. Therefore, Korea is the country that consumes the most pigs in the world.

Koreans like kimchi, Korea's signature dish, to take care of their health along with pork belly. Kimchi is a fermented cabbage mixed with dried red pepper powder that cleans the blood in our bodies and protects us from disease.
