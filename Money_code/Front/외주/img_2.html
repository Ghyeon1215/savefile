Culture

Korea is one of the countries that consumes a lot of alcohol. In particular, Soju is a Koreans' favorite because it is cheaper than beer and has an adequate alcohol content of about 20 percent.

There is a unique drinking culture in Korea. For example, when you drink with an older person, pour it into his glass with both hands and turn your head to drink.
